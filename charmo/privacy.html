Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Charmo iOS application ("App") and describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you use "Sign in with Apple", we collect the email address and display name provided by Apple for authentication purposes.
  • Chat Data: The conversations you have with the AI characters are securely transmitted to our servers to generate AI conversational responses in real-time.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide and improve the Services. Conversation data is temporarily processed by our AI backend models to craft intelligent responses. We do not sell your personal data to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Data Deletion

You can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated conversational data at any time either from inside the App settings or by directly contacting our support email securely.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@idakim.net.
