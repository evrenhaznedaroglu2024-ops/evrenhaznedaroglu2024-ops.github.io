Charmo - Support

Welcome to the Charmo support page. Charmo is your personal AI assistant, offering personalized conversations, professional consulting, and custom character creation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your Charmo Plus Auto-Renewing Subscription via your device's Apple ID Settings. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to view, change, or cancel your current plan.

What happens when my free limit is over?

Every new user has a trial messaging limit. Once your limit is exhausted, you'll need to upgrade to Charmo Plus to continue chatting limitlessly.
